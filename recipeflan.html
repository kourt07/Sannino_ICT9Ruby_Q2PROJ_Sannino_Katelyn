LECHE FLAN


10 eggs
1 cup fresh milk or evaporated milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup sugar
1 can condensed milk

Separate yolks, beat a fork/mixer, add condensed milk (mix thoroughly).
Pour milk and vanilla extract, mix well.
Caramelize the sugar. Use a Llanera (flan mold) and pour the sugar, after 5 mintues, add the egg mixture.
Cover with aluminum foil and steam.
