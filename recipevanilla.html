VANILLA MACARON


100 grams white granulated sugar
100 grams egg whites
105 grams almond flour
105 grams powdered sugar

Create the Italian meringue, and add the almonds.
Bake the Meringue using a parchment sheet, then start making the whipped filling (mix sugar and water until caramelized, heat hot cream in another pot, then merge when both are cooked immediately, add cubes of butter gradually while under the pot, is an ice bath. Whip the mixture once cooled for 15 minutes.
