MAJA BLANCA


½ cup water
½ cup cornstarch
1 cup coconut milk
¾ cup water
½ cup white sugar
¼ cup fresh sweet corn kernels

Mix cornstarch and water.
Combine coconut milk, water and sugar and dissolve. Soon after, add the kernels, then add the cornstarch in order to thicken the mixture.
When thickened, add in a container and wait for it to set.
