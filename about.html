ABOUT US


Gardenia is a website made to help intermediate bakers who have mastered general recipes. We offer recipes for cakes, puddings, and macarons. Refined in its manner, these delicate recipes take time and isn't impossible for beginners to make. The mastery, timing, and precise measurement each ingredient has can change a recipe greatly, and that is what we bakers aim to perfect. These desserts live to be a beauty in the mastery of precision, each sweet bite piece offering the thought and love of the baker.
